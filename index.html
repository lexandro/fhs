Welcome to my free hosted services page

On this page I try to collect some free services list that you could use as starting point for your proof of concept or startup project to save some money ;)

Source code hosting

  • Bitbucket - Unlimited private repositories, 5 users free
  • Assembla - Unlimited private repositories, 1 GB, unlimited users
  • GitLab - Unlimited private repositories, no size limit, free CI service for GitLab projects

Code quality and coverage analyzing

  • bitHound - Unlimited for open source repositories, all paid personal plans are $6/month (during the beta)

MongoDB

  • Mongolab - 500 MB, Rest API, full client driver support, monitoring, backups
  • MongoSoup - 500 MB, hosted in EU (EN/DE)
  • Morpheus - 5 GB, free during its beta test (at the moment)

Logging

  • logentries - 5 GB/months, 7 days retention
  • Papertrail - 5 GB/months (Increased with 100 MB/months), search 48 hrs, 7 days retention
  • Sentry - 7 days history, 250 events per day, no team (not logging, exception tracking)
  • Runbook - 2 monitors, 4 reactions, 5/30 mins checking in IFTTT style
  • Amplitude - user interaction analytics, 2 apps for free, real-time dashboard
  • Sumologic - 500 mb/day, 7 day retention, live streaming dashboards, logreduce analytics

Continuous Integration/Delivery

  • Shippable - Unlimited private builds, Github/Bitbucket integration, 1 build thread at the time, 2 cores 4 GB ram (unconfirmed)
  • Semaphore - 100 free private builds, Github/Bitbucket integration
  • Wercker - 2 concurrent builds, unlimited free repos, Github/Bitbucket integration (free during beta, no prices announced)
  • Codeship Parallel CI - 100 free private builds, 5 private repos, unlimited team members, Github/BitBucket/Cloud Foundry, Google App Engine integration
  • Snap CI - 1 build, no private repo support :(, only Github integration

Deployment

  • dploy.io - One free private repo, unlimited deployments, unlimited servers, unlimited users

Chatting

  • HipChat - 25000 msgs searchable, 5GB file size, unlimited integration
  • Gitter - 1 private room, 2 weeks history

Other

  • badge.fury.io - Auto generated free version badges
